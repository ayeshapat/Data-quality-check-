Interpretation of the above feature importance vs completeness graph:

The plot essentially tells us the trade off between how much information each feature is capturing and how much each feature is contributing to the overall performance of the model.

In the above graph, we do not see significant correlation between the feature importance for the ridge model and the completeness of the dataset. However we see a general trend of increase in feature importance with increase in completeness. But we see the concentration around the center of the plot. We observe that the features with completeness below 10%, have importances ranging from -10 to 20. We do see some rare features with feature importances above 20.

This most deifinitelt indicates that there is an insufficient data to actually capture the importance of the features and hence we see this behaviour in the plot.

In order to imporve the model, I will gather more data to establish a good feature importance relationship and engineer features with higher importances too. I will also ensure that the features are complete to the maximum extent at the data collection stage. One way to do this is to make most columns of data manadatory to fill. This will improve the model significantly.
